About Ireland

Ireland, known as the "Emerald Isle," is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and welcoming people. It continues to be a top travel destination, welcoming over 10 million tourists every year. It is popular due to its many vibrant cities, quaint villages, and many natural wonders. Ireland offers something for every traveler.

Fun Facts About the Emerald Isle

Wild Atlantic Way

Ireland has the longest coastal driving route in the world - over 2,500 miles stretching from Donegal in the North West to Cork in the South West

History

Ireland is home to the world's oldest pubs, one of the most famous manuscripts ever written, and where Viking's settled in 841AD

Culture

Irish culture is shaped by centuries of storytelling, folklore, instrumental music and dance

Language

Irish Gaelic remains as the official langauge of the country, taught in schools as a mandatory subject
